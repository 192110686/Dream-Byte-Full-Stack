One thing you must clarify FIRST

Ask him:

"Sir, can you give me one real completed bill and one partial return bill from your logbook? I need to understand exactly how you calculate rent."

Because the entire software depends on that calculation formula.

For example:

Formula A
Amount = Qty × Monthly Rate × Months
Formula B
Amount = Qty × Daily Rate × Days
Formula C
First month full charge
Extra days separate charge
Formula D
Minimum 30 days charge even if returned in 10 days

You should not assume. Get 2–3 real examples from K.S & CO. and build the calculation engine around their existing business practice.
