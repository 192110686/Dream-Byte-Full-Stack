Welcome to Our Cultural Fest

Welcome to College Cultural Fest 2026. This fest is a celebration of talent, creativity, teamwork, and student energy.

Students from different departments can participate in dance, music, drama, coding, art, photography, and many more exciting events.

This year, our college is focusing on student creativity and practical participation. Every student will get a chance to perform, compete, learn, and enjoy.

Fest Story

From Classroom to Stage

A cultural fest is not only about entertainment. It helps students become confident, expressive, and active. It also teaches leadership, time management, and teamwork.

Our previous entry fee was ₹500, but this year the updated student registration fee is ₹300.

The most awaited event of this year is Battle of Talents. Students can showcase their skills and win exciting prizes.

A famous quote says: Talent wins games, but teamwork wins championships.

College festivals create unforgettable memories. They bring students together, help them discover hidden talents, and create a platform where learning and celebration happen together.

Science club will also display formulas like H2O and maths club will explain equations like a2 + b2 = c2.
