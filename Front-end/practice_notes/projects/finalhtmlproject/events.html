Event Categories

Our cultural fest includes both cultural events and technical events. Students can choose events based on their interests and talents.

  • Dance Competition
  • Singing Competition
  • Drama Performance
  • Photography Contest
  • Coding Challenge
  • Food Stall Competition

Competition Rules

  1. Students must register before the deadline.
  2. Participants should bring their college ID card.
  3. Teams must report 30 minutes before the event.
  4. Judges' decision will be final.
  5. Participants must follow discipline inside the campus.

Final Countdown

  1. Attend the event
  2. Collect participant badge
  3. Reach the venue
  4. Practice performance
  5. Register your name
