Welcome to Smart Learning Academy

Smart Learning Academy is a training academy created for students who want to learn web development and full stack development from the basics.

Student Achievement Article

From Beginner to Web Developer

Riya Sharma joined Smart Learning Academy as a complete beginner. At first, she was nervous about coding, but slowly she became deeply interested in web development.

Her trainer told her, Practice is the strongest way to become confident in coding.

Learning coding is not about memorizing every tag or concept. It is about understanding the purpose, practicing regularly, making mistakes, correcting them, and building real projects.

Earlier her HTML score was 55%, but after regular practice, her new score became 92%.

She learned formulas like H2O and x2 + y2. The most important topic she enjoyed was HTML Forms.
